About Us

Founded in 2017 by Akshay Chaturvedi, Leverage Edu is empowering students across India to choose the best-matched career & higher education options, with one-touch access to personalised mentors and leading global universities. Leverage Edu has been lauded as India’s most trusted university admissions company.

Why Leverage Edu

Leverage Edu is a one-stop-shop for all your career-related needs - right from finding the best-fit university to helping you find accommodation at the best prices. With data-first approach at Leverage Edu, you get 100% customised services that resonate with your personal career needs.

What We Believe in

Each one of us has got something unique enough to be successful; we believe in bringing you closer to that ‘something’ and helping you leverage it!
